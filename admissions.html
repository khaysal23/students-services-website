Welcome to Admissions

Thank you for your interest in joining our university. This page provides guidance on the admission process and requirements.

Application Process

  1. Create an account on the admissions portal
  2. Complete the online application form
  3. Submit required academic documents
  4. Pay the application fee
  5. Review and submit your application
  6. Track your application status online

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Official academic transcripts
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • SAT or ACT scores
  • Personal statement
  • Two recommendation letters

Graduate Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution
  • Official undergraduate transcripts
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • GRE or GMAT scores (program dependent)
  • Statement of purpose
  • Three recommendation letters
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)

Important Deadlines

Application Type Deadline Decision Date
Early Decision November 1 December 15
Early Action November 15 January 15
Regular Decision January 15 April 1
Transfer Students March 1 May 15

Application Fee

The application fee is $75 for domestic students and $100 for international students. Fee waivers are available for eligible applicants.

International Students

  • TOEFL or IELTS scores
  • Certified English translations of transcripts
  • Financial capability documents
  • Valid passport copy
