Our Departments

The following departments are responsible for delivering various student services.

ICT Department

Provides technical support, manages student systems, login issues, online platforms, and digital services.

Academic Office

Handles course registration, examination results, academic records, and timetable management.

Library Services

Supports learning and research by providing books, digital resources, library membership, and clearance services.

Student Affairs

Manages student welfare, accommodation, guidance and counseling, and discipline.

Finance Department

Handles student fees, payments, financial statements, and finance-related services.

Registry Department

Responsible for registration, official letters, transcripts, and graduation records.
